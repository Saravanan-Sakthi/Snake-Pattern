Print the given matrix in the pattern of a snake from the diagonal

       1    2 *-- 3
      |   *    \
      *    \    *
      4     5    6
         \    *   |
          *    \  *
      7 *-- 8    9

    Sample input: 
      1 2 3
      4 5 6
      7 8 9
     Sample output:
      3 2 6 9 5 1 4 8 7
